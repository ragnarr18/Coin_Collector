START GAME:
    Run "Control3DProgram.py" to start the game.

CONTROLS:
    W:              move forward
    S:              move backwards
    A:              look left
    D:              look right
    UP arrow:       look up
    DOWN arrow:     look down
    Y:              toggle minimap (hint:   Enable this feature if the game is to hard, 
                                            the minimap shows you where the coins are and where the man is.)

HOW TO PLAY:
    You are stuck in a horror maze where a white blind man is trying to kill you.
    In order to survive and win the game you must collect all 5 coins that are in the maze.
    If the white man catches you before you collect the coins you lose!
